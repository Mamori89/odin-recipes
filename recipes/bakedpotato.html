Baked potato

Its potato season!

Lets go and make some baked potatos.

  • 4 (really) big potatos
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 1 bunch of spring onion
  • Rosemary
  • 500g of curd cheese
  • 3 table spoons of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°
  2. Wash your potatos and dry them - stab them a bunch of times with a fork.
  3. Lightly cover your potatos in oil and bake them for 50-60 minutes on a baking sheet.
  4. Prepare your curd cheese in a bowl
  5. Add your chopped parsley, spring onion and seasonings to the curd cheese and give it a good mix.
  6. Add some lemon juice to the mix and mix again.
  7. Put in seasonings to your taste and top it off on your potatos!
  8. Enjoy your baked potato!

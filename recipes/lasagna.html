Lasagna

Ingredients for 4 Portions:

  • 1 large Onion
  • 300 grams of minced meat
  • 200 grams of chopped mushrooms
  • 400 grams of tomatos (either fresh or canned)
  • 200 mililiters of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oregano
  • 12 Lasagna slices
  • A spoon full of butter
  • 1 fine chopped garlic
  • 100 grams Cheese of choice

Cooking-Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°
  2. Put in the chopped onions and garlic and stir until they get a bit brown
  3. After that add your minced meat and mushrooms and stir for another 5 minutes
  4. Add your fresh or canned tomatoes along with your milk and add your seasonings
  5. Evenly spread some butter inside your casserole dish and add a layer of lasagna slices
  6. Now add 1/3 of your filling as well as 1/3 of your cheese
  7. Repeat until all your filling is gone
  8. Bake it on 200° for 30 min and enjoy!

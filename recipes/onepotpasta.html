Ingredients for 4 Portions

  • 400 grams of fusilli (or your pasta of choice)
  • 150 grams of frozen peas
  • 1 can of corn
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 600 mililiters of veggie-stock
  • 1 can (400 mililiters)of sieved tomatoes
  • 300 grams of cream cheese
  • 100 grams of parmesan
  • Italian seasonings
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley for garnish
  1. Wash and clean your bellpeppers and chop them into small cubes
  2. Put your pasta of choice in a casserole dish and evenly distribute the ingredients
  3. Put the casserole dish in the oven at 200Â° for 30 minutes
  4. Let it cool for a bit, take it out and mix it well.
  5. Garnish with some parsley and enjoy!
